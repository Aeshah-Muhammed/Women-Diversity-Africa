About the conference

Goals

Our goal at Women Diversity Africa is to ensure the diversity of women as a means of providing sustainable development in the workplace.

Aim and Objective

Our aims and objectives are;

  • To showcase the power of African women,
  • To ensure diversity in the workplace,
  • To help women develop confidence in themselves and their abilities,
  • To ensure that change happens in the workplace in terms of diversity,
  • Ensure all these factors foster sustainable development in the African workplace environment.

    • Background

    Women diversity in the workplace is the practice of including and involving women from a range of different social and ethnic backgrounds in the workplace.

    Problem Definition

    After thorough research, the team understands the challenges African women face in the workplace, and how this plays a major role in the sustainable development of organizations, and society.

    These challenges range from gender gaps in organizations, lower chances of promotability, gender-based stereotypes, greater scrutiny at leadership roles, and many more.

    Solution

    Women Diversity Africa is a conference that seeks to showcase the strength of African women, raise conversations regarding the diversity of women in the workplace, discuss how to improve women's inclusion, and build a community of women to promote allies, across all sectors.

    [ Over View ]

    Women Diversity Africa is a conference that aims to further bring to light the importance of diversity in Africa through showcases, conversations, and an active community.

    This event’s theme seeks to drive a conversation on the importance of diversity in the workplace, especially in women inclusion, as a tool for sustainable development.

    Duration

    This is its first conference and it is scheduled to hold in January, 2023

    Target Audience

    Our target audience is everybody, not just women. We seek to showcase, so the world must watch.

    Program Outcome

    We hope that after this conference, the world will appreciate the power of women more, and everyone starts to work towards making a positive change in terms of women diversity in the workplace.

    [ Sponsorship/Partnership ]

    Sponsors and partners are welcome from across all professional sectors. Kindly visit our website for the sponsorship/partnership details, and also for donations.

    Criteria for Selecting Speakers

    There is a standard every speaker must meet, and they are;

    • Every speaker at the conference must be a woman.
    • Must be a front liner in their sector.
    • Must be willing to share their story.
    • Must be passionate about making positive changes in terms of diversity in the workplace.
    Team Members

    There are various teams under the wing of Women Diversity Africa, and they are;

    • Program organization
    • Technical and media
    • Registration
    • Legal
    • Sponsorship/Partnership

    Team members will be renewed, as registration links will be sent out yearly for interested women to apply.

    Stakeholders

    Women Diversity Africa has stakeholders different professional sectors. These are committed and seasoned industry experts.

    These sectors include;

    • Technology Sector
    • Health Sector
    • Engineering and Manufacturing sector
    • Government sector
    • Agriculture sector
    • Media (Information and Communication) sector

    Stakeholders manage their sector’s community, team leads, and volunteers across all continents.
